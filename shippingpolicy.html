Legend's Tech - Shipping Policy

Shipping Policy

At Legend’s Tech, we’re dedicated to getting your order to you as quickly and safely as possible. Please review our shipping policies below for full details.

Processing Time

All orders are processed within 1-3 business days. If we are experiencing a high volume of orders, shipments may be delayed by a few days. You will receive a notification if your shipment is delayed beyond our standard window.

Shipping Rates & Delivery Estimates

Shipping charges for your order will be calculated and displayed at checkout. We offer various shipping methods depending on your location and preference. Estimated delivery time is typically 3-7 business days within the U.S.

Shipment Confirmation & Order Tracking

You will receive a shipment confirmation email once your order has shipped containing your tracking number(s). The tracking number will be active within 24 hours.

Damages

Legend’s Tech is not liable for any products damaged or lost during shipping. If your order arrives damaged, please contact the shipment carrier to file a claim. Be sure to save all packaging materials and damaged goods before filing.

International Shipping Policy

We currently do not ship outside the U.S. If international shipping becomes available, we’ll update this section with new information.
